Our Journey

In 2010, Next Travel Pvt Ltd was founded by our visionary CEO and Founder, Ravi. Our primary goal was to provide personalized travel experiences, and with the contribution of talents like Miss Ruvini Rangathara, we rapidly expanded. Guided by Ravi's vision and a commitment to excellence, we initially focused on Sri Lanka, where we became a trusted online travel partner for tailor-made tours. Today, with over 200 dedicated team members, we remain firm in our belief that personalized travel is the key to savoring a destination. Miss Ruvini Rangathara plays a vital role in ensuring our travel packages, including guides, vehicles, hotels, and rooms, enhance your journey. We champion equality with a workforce comprising 75% women and empower the younger generation to lead us into a promising future. At Next Travel Pvt Ltd, we're here to guide you through the world's adventures.

Vision

To become the world's foremost provider of tailor-made travel experiences, setting the standard for personalized adventures across the globe.

Mission

- We are dedicated to crafting unforgettable journeys that cater to the unique needs and desires of our travelers.

- We will continuously innovate and adapt to ensure that our travel experiences remain unparalleled and exceed the expectations of our customers.

- Our mission is to make every journey with Next Travel Pvt Ltd a memorable and enriching experience, embracing the diverse wonders of the world.


The Team & Dedication

Next Travel Pvt Ltd boasts a dynamic and dedicated team comprising 200 members stationed across the globe in various key offices. Our team is our greatest pride and the most valuable asset at Next Travel Pvt Ltd. Our representatives are not only professional and friendly but also filled with boundless energy and unwavering dedication. They will always welcome our customers with a sincere and heartwarming smile!


Why Choose Us?


1. Customization: Next Travel Pvt Ltd provides tailor-made travel plans, ensuring that each itinerary is uniquely designed to meet the preferences and requirements of individual travelers.

2. Local Expertise: Benefit from the in-depth local knowledge of Next Travel's teams, who can provide valuable insights, recommendations, and guidance for a truly immersive travel experience.

3. Time and Cost Efficiency: The system helps optimize your travel plans, saving you time and money by offering the most cost-effective options for accommodation, transportation, and activities.

4. Convenience: The system offers a one-stop platform for all your travel needs, including booking flights, accommodations, transportation, and activities. This streamlines the entire planning process.
